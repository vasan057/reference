Cargoji

Logistics aggregator app bridging vehicle owners with customers and loads.

ABOUT

Cargoji is a revolutionary new improvement to Logistics systems in India. Within our Cities the transport and logistics is a closed circuit with low supply and high fluctuating demand. In order to bridge this gap, CargoJi is providing a platform for Cargo and Truck movers to participate in easing this deficit.
