About Us

Falconnect Technologies is an Angel funded, Technology Startup based out of Chennai. Mobile & Web Application Development are our primary service offerings. We are a Technology Savvy Company always using the cutting edge trends in the industry to deliver the best to our clients. We are always curious about what’s around the corner and how we might be able to leverage it to benefit the Application Development that we are engaged in.

Our Work

We do the whole nine yards, starting from Design, Development, Testing and Maintenance. Developing high quality Digital Products and offering Top-Notch solutions for Mobile and Web is our key takeaway. We Build native Mobile Applications (iOS, Android, Windows Phone) as well as advanced HTML5 Applications. We offer accompanying front-end and back-end web development in various contemporary technologies. Our Mobile & Web App developers understand the pain-points of our customers trying to create and deploy reliable multi-platform solutions that can help them on a long run. We bring the right mix of strategy, user experience design and engineering expertise to every single project. Our unique product design and mobile application development processes drive the design and execution of innovative digital experiences that help clients in various domains to be successful.
