Dealerplus.in

Dealerplus is a revolutionary Dealer Management System built from scratch for a prominent client which caters to used car dealers in India.

ABOUT

Our revolutionary DMS is here to make your job easier than ever with our ground-breaking technology and our high domain expertise. It has never been easier than now to add/manage your stock inventory and posting them on multiple platforms with just one click of a button. Our latest innovation in this field of used car market is bound to be extremely beneficial and time saving for you, while your time on advertising is cut down to nearly 95% also your sales numbers will drastically improve as we are partnered with almost all the major classified and marketplace platforms. With just one click your stock is displayed to millions of potentials buyers!

POWER PACKED

Dealerplus has a whole gamut of functionalities ranging from Daily Dashboard, Multi channel sales, Inventory Management, Contact Management, Leads Management and Scoring, Business Page, Inventory Funding and Loans, Networking with other dealers, Invoices and Quotes, Listing Alerts, Marketing and much more.

DASHBOARD AND REPORTING

An extremely user friendly dashboard where dealers can monitor and assess performance of their inventory. The transparent reporting module generates all the information needed to analyze their business at any given time.

INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

Adding your stock, and keeping track of them while maintaining absolute control over their sales cycle and services along with your customers is made extremely easy with Dealer Plus. A complete end to end top notch software solution for car dealers made by experts of this industry.

FUNDING AND LOANS

From front-end ticketing to back-end collateral management for availing Funding for your Inventory, Working Capital for your Business and Loans for your Customers, DealerPlus leverages scale, support and best-in-class technology to ensure precise data, faster transaction times, and complete servicing for all the requests using innovative automation systems.
